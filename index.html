Good to know

Frequently asked questions

Is ClassTrack really free?
Yes. All the core tools — attendance, bunk calculator, timetable, exams, GPA, focus, habits and tasks — are free forever. Pro unlocks unlimited AI and a few power features.
How does the bunk calculator work?
You set an attendance target (say 75%). ClassTrack tracks your present/absent classes and tells you exactly how many you can skip while staying above target — or how many to attend to recover.
Can the AI really read my timetable from a photo?
Yep. Snap a picture or type it in plain words, and the Gemini-powered assistant proposes a full schedule you can review and add to your calendar in one tap.
Does my data sync across devices?
Your account is backed up securely to the cloud, so your subjects, attendance and grades follow you across your phones.
Which platforms are supported?
ClassTrack is built for Android and iOS from a single, polished codebase, with a home-screen widget for your day at a glance.
