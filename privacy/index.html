Overview

ClassTrack ("we", "us") is a student productivity app for attendance, timetables, exams, grades and study tools. We believe your data is yours. This policy explains what we collect, why, and the choices you have.

1. Information we collect

Information you provide

  • Account details — your email address and, for password sign-in, an encrypted password.
  • Your study data — subjects, class schedules, attendance marks, exams, grades, tasks, notes, habits and expenses you enter.
  • Messages — anything you send us through support or the AI assistant.

Information collected automatically

  • Device & usage data — app version, device type, crash logs and basic analytics used to keep the app working well.
  • Subscription status — whether you have an active Pro plan (managed via the app stores / RevenueCat).

2. How we use your information

  • To provide core features — syncing your subjects, attendance and grades across your devices.
  • To send reminders and notifications you enable (classes, tasks, exams, habits).
  • To power the AI study assistant when you use it.
  • To process subscriptions and prevent abuse.
  • To improve reliability and fix bugs.

We do not sell your personal data.

3. AI features & photo import

When you use the AI assistant — including importing a timetable from a photo — the text or image you submit is sent to our AI provider (such as Google Gemini) to generate a response. Only send content you're comfortable sharing for this purpose. We use these features to build your schedule and give study tips, not to identify you.

4. Who we share data with

We share limited data only with service providers that help us run ClassTrack, under agreements that protect your information:

  • Google Firebase — authentication, database and hosting.
  • AI provider (e.g. Google Gemini) — to process AI requests you initiate.
  • RevenueCat & the app stores — to manage subscriptions and payments.
  • Web3Forms — to deliver messages you send from our support form.

We may also disclose information if required by law or to protect rights and safety.

5. Data retention

We keep your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete a subject or your account, the associated data (including schedules and attendance) is removed from our active systems, subject to reasonable backup and legal-retention periods.

6. Your rights & choices

  • Access & export — view and export your data from within the app.
  • Correct & delete — edit your entries or delete your account at any time.
  • Notifications — turn reminders on or off in Settings.
  • Regional rights — depending on where you live (e.g. GDPR/CCPA), you may have additional rights; contact us to exercise them.

7. Security

We use industry-standard measures — encryption in transit, secure authentication and access controls — to protect your data. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we work hard to safeguard your information.

8. Children

ClassTrack is intended for students aged 13 and older (or the minimum age of digital consent in your country). We don't knowingly collect data from children under that age. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us and we'll remove it.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Material changes will be announced in-app or by email, and we'll update the date at the top.

10. Contact us

Questions or requests about your privacy? Email support@classtracks.app or use our support page.
